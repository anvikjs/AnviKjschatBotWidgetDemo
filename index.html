School Overview

1. Admission Policy

Admissions open: June – July every year

Based on seat availability and age criteria

Minimum age for Nursery: 3+ years (as of March 31)

Documents required:
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport-size photos (2)
  • Previous school report card (if applicable)
  • Address proof
  • Transfer certificate for grades above LKG

Admission criteria: Previous academic records + interview for classes 1–12

2. Fee Policy / Structure
  • LKG – Grade 5: ₹30,000/year
  • Grades 6–8: ₹40,000/year
  • Grades 9–10: ₹50,000/year
  • Grades 11–12 (Science): ₹70,000/year

Transport fee: ₹5,000/year

Annual activities fee: ₹3,000/year

Fees payable quarterly by 10th; late fee ₹100/day

Refund policy: Admission fee non-refundable; tuition refundable if withdrawal before session.

3. Curriculum & Syllabus (Science Stream)

Grade 9–10: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, English, Social Studies

Grade 11–12 (Science):
  • Physics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Electricity, Optics
  • Chemistry: Organic, Inorganic, Physical Chemistry
  • Biology: Genetics, Human Physiology, Ecology
  • Mathematics: Calculus, Algebra, Probability
  • Optional: Computer Science
4. School Timings

Monday – Friday:

  • LKG–5: 8:30 AM – 2:00 PM
  • Grades 6–10: 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Grades 11–12: 8:00 AM – 3:30 PM

No classes on Saturday

Late entry after 8:15 AM

Early departure requires written permission

5. Attendance & Leave Policy
  • Minimum 85% attendance to appear in exams
  • Sick leave: up to 7 days/term with doctor’s note
  • Casual leave: 5 days/term
  • Leave >2 days requires application
  • Medical leave requires doctor's certificate
  • 10 days of absence without notice → removal from roll
6. Examination & Evaluation Policy
  • LKG – 5: Continuous assessment + quarterly exams
  • Grades 6–10: Half-yearly + Annual exams
  • Grades 11–12: Pre-board + Board exams
  • Minimum passing: 40%
  • Re-exam for medical reasons
  • Includes theory, practicals, internal assessments
7. Uniform Policy

Summer: White shirt + navy shorts/pants + tie (grades 6+)

Winter: Navy blazer + school tie + black shoes

Sports: White t-shirt + navy shorts + white shoes

ID cards mandatory; neat hairstyle

8. Discipline Policy
  • No bullying, physical fights, abusive language
  • Mobile phones not allowed (confiscated for 1 week)
  • Repeated misconduct → suspension
9. Transport Policy
  • Buses operate on fixed routes
  • Reach stop 5 minutes early
  • Misbehavior → suspension from transport
  • Transport fee included in school fees
10. Safety & Security
  • Parents must carry ID card during pickup
  • CCTV in classrooms, corridors, buses
  • Fire drills every 3 months
11. Notices / Circulars

  • Science exhibition: 5 October (grades 6–12)
